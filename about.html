About Us

Janan Schools is a privately owned family of institutions, comprising two primary schools and two secondary schools, founded in 2003.

Our schools draw inspiration from a strong religious foundation and are proudly named in honor of Archbishop Janani Jakaliya Luwum, who served as Archbishop of the Church of Uganda from 1974 to 1977.
